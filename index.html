HELLO EVERYONE!

Carmen Segovia is an artist illustrator based in Barcelona. After a first period of set design studies she specializes in illustration at Llotja and Eina Art Schools in Barcelona. In 2002 her illustrations were selected at the Bologna Children Books Fair and since then she has not stopped developing illustrated narrative projects.

She contributes to international press and publications, while regularly signing illustrated books. She also develops her personal drawing series, short comic stories and other collaborative projects. She teaches Illustrated Narrative in Master in Creative Illustration at BAU Design College.

ABOUT

I was born in Barcelona, in 1978. After a first period of scenography studies, I specialized in illustration. I combines publishing collaborations with the creation of illustrated books. At the same time, I develops personal drawing and narrative projects. Lengua de lava is my last solo exhibition at Galeria Vertigo in Mexico City.
